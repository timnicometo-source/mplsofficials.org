NASO Information

NASO group membership is available for MOA members. MSHSL insurance is provided through dues paid to MSHSL. NASO is more than the additional insurance coverage, and members are encouraged to review the benefits available through NASO.

If you are interested in NASO group membership, please contact the MOA representative listed below. Group rate information and payment instructions may change from year to year.

Benefits of Membership

Insurance

  • Liability insurance up to $6,000,000.
  • Covers all sports for a full 12 months, not only when working MSHSL contests.

Discounts

  • Hotel discount opportunities.
  • Discounts from officiating apparel and equipment vendors.
  • Auto rental discounts.
  • Publication discounts from NASO and Referee Magazine.

Other Benefits

  • Referee Magazine as part of membership.
  • Access to NASO member resources and educational information.
  • Potential tax-deductible expense. Officials should consult their own tax advisor.

Annual Availability

NASO group membership may only be offered during a specific window each year. Check with MOA leadership or the treasurer for current availability, rates, and payment deadlines.
